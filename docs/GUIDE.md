# PrismOS — User Guide

PrismOS is a **38-game offline gaming platform** built for Pass & Play on a single device. No accounts, no downloads — install once and play anywhere.

**Live app:** https://shamikhahmed.github.io/PrismOS

---

## Getting started

1. Open PrismOS in Safari (iPhone) or any modern browser.
2. **Select your device** — iPhone model, iPad, or Mac/Browser. Safe areas adjust automatically.
3. Complete the welcome flow: name, age, gender theme preference.
4. Browse the game library and tap any game to play.
5. **Install:** Share → Add to Home Screen for full-screen PWA mode.

---

## Pass & Play

Most games support **multiple players on one device**:

1. Enter player names at game start.
2. Hand the phone to each player for private reveals (roles, votes, answers).
3. The **Drama Engine** tracks tension and trust across the session.
4. Results and XP are saved locally.

Perfect for parties, road trips, and family game night.

## Profile settings

Access from the profile tab:

- Change device model (safe area recalibration)
- Switch theme
- View XP, rank, and achievements
- Reset progress (clears localStorage)

---

## Offline play

After first load, PrismOS works **fully offline**:

- Service worker caches all game assets
- No internet required for any game session
- localStorage persists XP, settings, and suspended games

---

## Tips

- Pick your exact iPhone model on first launch — Dynamic Island and notch spacing are calibrated per device.
- Use Tournament Mode for game-night brackets across different titles.
- Enable music for rhythm games; disable in quiet settings.
- QR transfer works device-to-device without cloud — useful for continuing a long Heist session.

---

## Troubleshooting

| Issue | Fix |
|-------|-----|
| Games won't load | Clear Safari cache, revisit live URL, wait for loader to finish |
| Wrong safe area | Profile → Device → re-select your model |
| Music won't play | Tap music indicator; iOS may require a user tap first |
| Progress lost | Data is per-browser; re-installing PWA may reset — export via QR before reset |
