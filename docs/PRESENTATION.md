## Slide 2 — Insight

Party games need a host, rules, and props.

Phones are already in everyone's pocket — but most "mobile games" need accounts, ads, and Wi‑Fi.

**PrismOS** is the console you already own.

---

## Slide 3 — What it is

A single-page PWA gaming platform:

- **38 games** across deception, reflex, strategy, party, competitive
- **Pass & Play** — private reveals on one screen
- **Fully offline** after first load
- **No accounts** — localStorage progression

## Slide 5 — Pass & Play flow

1. Add player names
2. Hand device for secret roles / votes
3. Drama Engine tracks tension
4. XP awarded locally

*One phone. Six players. No app store.*

---

## Slide 6 — Engines

- **Drama Engine** — tension, trust, paranoia metrics
- **Director Engine** — adaptive pacing and difficulty
- **Web Audio API** — synthesized music, zero audio files
- **Haptics** — Vibration API for impact moments
