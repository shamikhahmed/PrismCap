## Description
PrismCap packs **39** multiplayer and solo games into one offline-first PWA — Spy Hunt, Chaos Cards, chess, reflex drills, and more.

Play on a single device with friends (pass-and-play), track XP and streaks in Smart Hub, and browse the full library without an account.

• No login / no ads / no Capricorn cloud saves  
• Works Add-to-Home-Screen on iOS and Android  
• Demo profile: open with `?demo=1`
