Why We Built This
The Problem
📱

App store games want accounts and ads

Sign up, subscribe, watch ads — before you even play with friends.

👥

Pass-and-play is rare on mobile

Party games need props, hosts, and Wi‑Fi. Phones should be enough.

🔥

Janky performance kills the fun

Heavy frameworks drain battery and lag during fast reflex games.

☁️

Cloud-dependent — dead at the campsite

No signal? No game night. Offline should be the default.

Our Answer
The Solution
A pocket arcade — 39 games, one device, zero internet.
🎮
39 games in one PWA
Deception, reflex, board, puzzle — all offline after first load.
👥
Pass & Play for 1–10+ players
Private reveals, handoffs, bot fill — one phone, whole party.
🔒
No account. No server. Ever.
XP, settings, suspended games — all in localStorage on your device.
